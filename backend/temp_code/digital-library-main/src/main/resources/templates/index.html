Overview

A Library Management System (LMS) is an application that streamlines library operations: cataloging, issuing, returning and tracking resources. It helps librarians and users find and manage materials more efficiently.

Our LMS focuses on:

OTP-based student & faculty login
Admin-controlled approvals & book management
Year / Semester filtered catalog

Libraries are evolving hubs — combining physical stacks with digital access to support education, research, and lifelong learning.
